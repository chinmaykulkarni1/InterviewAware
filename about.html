About Me

Founder: Chinmay Kulkarni: LinkedIn

Chinmay Kulkarni, current fulltime MBA student at Emory University - Goizueta Business School, has nearly 4 years of industry experience as a hardware engineer, working for a startup innovating on indoor location tracking technology, and at Qualcomm in Silicon Valley, working on integrating key electronic components for the iPhone15.

About InterviewAware

Welcome to InterviewAware! The world's first engineering interview prep service. We understand that interviewing is a difficult and messy ordeal, going from source to source, looking up endless online videos and message boards with suspect interview insights. Our focus is to bring a high level of transparency to the engineering interview process, by practicing technical engineering skills, testing design fundamentals, and refreshing even the brightest of engineers on obscure topics that were not covered since college.

We know that the cool thing to study is coding, and likewise, there are several excellent resources out there for studying and brushing up on computer science fundamentals... why can't engineering disciplines have the same resources? We believe to land a dream job, in Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, or other engineering disciplines, that we can take the anxiety out of interview preparation to make everyone place their best foot forward.

There are numerous issues with the scattered sources that engineers use for interview preparation. YouTube, often has videos that are far too long, or often even in a different language. Wikipedia likes to display educational material in a confusing maze of hyperlinks and texts. Finally, sources like Reddit and Glassdoor use a crowdsourcing strategy that often leads to incomplete or incorrect interview questions and related materials.

It's a shame that engineering interview prep is highly convoluted and disaggregated. This is the vision and the issues that InterviewAware wishes to solve.

Circuit Theory

Circuit theory is critical component of the interview preparation process, since it forms the basis of a majority of electronic devices. Interview questions around analyzing, designing, and troubleshooting electronic systems are extremely common and serve as some of the more difficult types of interview questions. However, proficiency in concepts surrounding circuit theory will create the confidence to tackle even the toughest of problems.

  • What is Circuit Theory?

Power Electronics

Power systems and the respective design is a crucial component of the interview process - as a main challenge to designing efficient electronic systems. Questions around power systems are very common, as power restrictions are very common in all types of engineering applications. Specialization around specific roles in power electronics have emerged, each for their different application and skills. Here, we will discuss some of the critical components of electrical engineering power architecture and understand how to optimize optimizing power transmission, conversion, and utilization for all types of electrical devices.
